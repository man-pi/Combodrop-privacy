Privacy & Support

ComboDrop: Number Puzzle

Overview

ComboDrop is a puzzle game developed by Federico Mantegazza. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

ComboDrop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or any third-party services that collect user information.

Local Data

The app stores game data (high scores, settings, and progress) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Game Center

ComboDrop integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Game Center account information.

Children's Privacy

ComboDrop does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The game is suitable for all ages.
